title: "RootEncoder"
description: "An Android SDK for encoding and streaming live video and audio to media servers using RTMP, RTSP, SRT, and UDP."
---

RootEncoder is a powerful Android streaming library written in Java/Kotlin. It handles video and audio capture, hardware-accelerated encoding, real-time OpenGL filtering, and delivery over RTMP, RTSP, SRT, and UDP — all in a single dependency.
